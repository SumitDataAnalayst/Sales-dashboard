#  Sales Dashboard (Excel)

##  Overview

This project showcases an interactive sales dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions such as time, region, products, and customers. The dashboard enables quick decision-making through dynamic visualizations and KPIs.

---

## Objectives

* Monitor overall sales and profit performance
* Identify trends over time
* Analyze regional and product-level performance
* Provide actionable insights for business growth

---

## Dataset

The dataset includes:

* Order Date
* Sales
* Profit
* Quantity
* Region
* Product Category and Sub-category
* Customer details

---

## Dashboard Features

* KPI metrics (Total Sales, Total Profit, Order Count, Profit Margin)
* Pivot tables and pivot charts
* Interactive slicers (Region, Category, Date)
* Monthly and yearly trend analysis
* Top-performing products and regions

---

## Tools & Techniques Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers & Timelines
* Data Cleaning and Transformation
* Basic formulas (SUM, COUNT, IF, etc.)

## Key Insights

* Sales show strong seasonal trends across months
* A few regions contribute the majority of revenue
* Some products generate high sales but low profit
* Top customers significantly impact total revenue

---

## Business Recommendations

* Focus on high-margin products
* Improve performance in low-performing regions
* Leverage top customers for repeat business
* Optimize pricing for low-profit items
